Posts here sit at the intersection of halacha, Jewish learning, and the texture of daily practice. The register is Talmudic — meaning positions get argued, not just stated. Where the sources are ambiguous, that ambiguity is preserved rather than resolved for the reader's convenience. Where the author doesn't know, the author says so.
