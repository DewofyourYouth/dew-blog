---
title: "Heritage & Identity"
description: "Genealogical research and family history: census records, ship manifests, photographs, and the interpretive gap between what documents say and what they mean."
---

Genealogical research, family history, and the documentary record of the Shore and Saranduk families. The method is archival: census records, ship manifests, photographs, and whatever else survived. Documents are shown before they are interpreted, and the gap between the two is treated as data rather than a problem to be resolved. Uncertainty about dates, names, and identities is recorded, not hidden.
